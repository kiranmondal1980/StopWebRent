Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really $0 monthly fees forever?

Yes. We utilize 'Static Site Architecture' (JAMstack). This allows your site to be hosted on Enterprise-grade global CDNs like Netlify, Vercel, or Cloudflare Pages. These platforms offer generous free tiers (usually up to 100GB bandwidth) which is enough for 99% of small businesses.

How do I update my website?

We connect your website to a specific Google Sheet. You simply type new text, prices, or paste image links into that sheet. The Titan Engine detects the change and rebuilds your site automatically in seconds. It is as easy as using Excel.

What happens if I want to leave?

You can't be "locked in" because you own the code. Upon completion, we deliver the full HTML, CSS, and Javascript source code to you. You can take that zip file and host it anywhere you want. You are the owner, not a renter.

Do I need to pay for a domain name?

Yes. You must purchase your domain (e.g., yourname.com) from a registrar like GoDaddy or Namecheap. This typically costs ~$15/year. We do not mark this up; you pay them directly.

Is it secure for taking payments?

Extremely. Since we don't store customer data in a hackable database on your server, the risk is minimal. For payments, we integrate directly with Stripe or PayPal's secure checkout pages, so they handle the sensitive credit card processing, not your site.

Can I add a blog later?

Yes. The Titan Engine is modular. We can activate the Blog Module at any time. You will write your blog posts in the Google Sheet using simple formatting, and they will publish as SEO-optimized pages automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is it possible to have $0 Monthly Fees?

It comes down to architecture. Traditional sites (WordPress, Wix) require a heavy server running 24/7 to process databases, which costs money. We build "Static" sites. They are pre-rendered and live on the Global Edge (CDNs like Netlify or Vercel). Because they don't require active server processing, these providers offer generous free tiers that 99% of small businesses never exceed.

Do I need coding skills to update my site?

Absolutely not. We utilize a "Spreadsheet CMS." We connect your website to a private Google Sheet. To update a price, change a photo, or add a blog post, you simply type it into the spreadsheet. The website updates automatically. If you can use Excel, you can manage a Titan site.

What if I stop paying you?

You can't "stop paying us" because you don't pay us monthly! Once you pay the setup fee, the code is yours. If StopWebRent.com disappeared tomorrow, your website would keep running perfectly. You own the asset, not a lease.

Is it really faster than WordPress?

Yes, by a significant margin. WordPress sites have to "think" (query a database) every time a user visits. Titan sites are "ready." They load in ~0.1 seconds because there is no database bottleneck. This is crucial for Google Rankings (Core Web Vitals).

What about my Domain Name (www.mybusiness.com)?

You purchase that directly from a registrar like GoDaddy or Namecheap (usually ~$15/year). You retain full control of your domain. We simply point it to your new ultra-fast server. We do not markup domain prices.

Is it secure from hackers?

It is significantly more secure than WordPress. 90% of website hacks happen via "SQL Injection" (hacking the database). Since Titan sites use Zero-DB Architecture (No Database), there is literally nothing for a hacker to break into. Your site is a digital fortress.

Can I add an Online Store later?

Yes. The Titan Engine is modular. We can add "Buy Buttons" connected to Stripe or a "WhatsApp Cart" at any time. We don't take a commission on your sales.

What if I need a major design change later?

For content updates (text/images), you use your Google Sheet for free forever. If you want a structural design overhaul (e.g., adding a new page layout), we charge a one-time "Expansion Fee" based on the work required. No contracts.

Does this work for SEO?

Google loves speed. Because Titan sites load instantly and are mobile-perfect, they often outrank heavy, slow competitor sites. We also include Schema Markup and Open Graph tags by default.

Who is this NOT for?

The Titan Engine is perfect for Service Businesses, Local Biz, and Boutique E-commerce. It is NOT designed for massive portals like Amazon, Facebook, or complex membership sites requiring user logins.
